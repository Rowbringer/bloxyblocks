Parents - Important Information About BloxyRocks

Parents Information

About BloxyRocks

BloxyRocks is a kid-friendly online platform where children can exercise their creativity in a moderated environment. We are committed to providing a safe, fun, and engaging experience for young players.

Safety Features

  • Strict content moderation and filtering
  • Parent controls and account management
  • No real-world personal information sharing
  • Monitored chat and communication
  • Regular security updates and maintenance

Parent Account Control

Set up a Parent Account to monitor and manage your child's gaming experience. With parental controls, you can:

  • Manage spending and virtual currency limits
  • View your child's in-game activity
  • Control who your child can communicate with
  • Set play time limits
  • Review game content and ratings

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. BloxyRocks does not share personal information with third parties. All data is securely stored and protected with industry-standard encryption.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about BloxyRocks, our parent support team is here to help.
