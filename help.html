Help & Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Register" button on the login panel. Fill in your username, password, and email address. Once confirmed, your account will be ready to use!
How do I get ROBUX?
You can earn ROBUX by playing games, completing tasks, or purchase them directly through the catalog. Check the "Buy ROBUX" section for more details.
Can I create my own games?
Yes! If you're a Builders Club member, you can create games using our building tools. Visit Builders Club for more information.
How do I customize my avatar?
Visit the Catalog page to browse and purchase avatar items. You can mix and match accessories, clothing, and other items to create your unique look.
Is BloxyRocks safe for kids?
Yes! We have strict content moderation, chat filtering, and parental controls. Visit the Parents section for more safety information.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
Click the "Report" button on any user's profile or use the in-game report feature. Our moderation team will review reports promptly.
Why can't I access certain games?
Some games may have age restrictions or may be under maintenance. Try refreshing the page or contacting support if you continue to have issues.
How do I join Builders Club?
Visit the Builders Club page to see membership options and pricing. Click "Join Builders Club Now" to get started!

Still Need Help?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
